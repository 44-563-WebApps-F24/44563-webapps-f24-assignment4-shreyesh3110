Shreyesh Chennagouni presents a Greek play by Sophocles

OEDIPUS AT COLONOS - SOPHOCLES

OEDIPUS

Get thee gone! I speak their mind as well—and dog not me Beleaguering mine appointed dwelling-place!

CHORUS

These men shall witness—for thy word is naught; And for thy spiteful answer to thy friends, If once I seize thee—

OEDIPUS

Who shall seize on me Without the will of my protectors here?
